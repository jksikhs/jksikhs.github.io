Gurdwaras in Jammu

Listed in alphabetical order of primary city or town, then locality name.

Includes Jammu city as well as the province of Jammu

Gurdwara Sri Guru Singh Sabha

Guru Nanak Nagar, Jammu

One of the leading and busiest Gurdwaras of Jammu suburbs west of Tawi

Gurdwara Baba Fateh Singh Ji

Gandhi Nagar, Jammu

Gurdwara building was expanded in the second decade of twenty-first century to cater to sangat from Gandhi Nagar. It is situated less than half a kilometer from Gol Market

Gurdwara Kalgidhar Sahib

Rehari, Jammu

Located near the Rehari locality on the busy national highway

Dera Baba Banda Bahadur

Reasi

Gurdwara Sahib associated with the history of Baba Banda Singh Bahadur who was from Jammu. Situated in Reasi District on the banks of River Chenab, Jammu and Kashmir 185201

Gurdwara Sahib Rocha Singh Pura

Batote

Gurdwara Sahib Rocha Singh Pura is situated near the town of Batote, in district Doda, Jammu and Kashmir, 182143

Gurdwara Dhehri Sahib

Poonch

Gurdwara Sahib with a history of over 200 years is situated along the Poonch-Rawalkot highway
