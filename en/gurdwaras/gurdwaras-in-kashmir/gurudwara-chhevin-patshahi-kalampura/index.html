Gurudwara Chhevin Patshahi (Kalampura)

Gurdwara Chhevin Patshahi (also known as Thara Sahib or Chhati Patshahi) of Kalampura is situated near at a historic location near the village of Singhpora Kalan. The locality of Kalampura is is at a distance of approximately 12km from the Baramulla town centre and is located on high ground. The Gurdwara compound contains many chinar (Oriental plane) trees, and is surrounded by some of the best apple orchards in the region.

Guru Hargobind Sahib are said to have visited this place, and a well in the Gurdwara compound is said to be Their gift to the village. The Gurdwara building was renovated in the first decade of 21st century, and includes a langar hall and staff quarters.
