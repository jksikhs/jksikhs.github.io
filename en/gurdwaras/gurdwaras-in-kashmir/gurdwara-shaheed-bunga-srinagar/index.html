Gurdwara Shaheed Bunga Sahib (Srinagar)

The Gurdwara is located in Baghat locality of Srinagar, Kashmir at a distance of 6 km  from Tourist Reception centre

This Gurdwara was constructed in the memory of Sikh Soldiers of Maharaja Ranjit Singh during the Sikh rule in Kashmir
