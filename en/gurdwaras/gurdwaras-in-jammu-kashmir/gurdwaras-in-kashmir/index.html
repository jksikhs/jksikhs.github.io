Gurdwara Shaheed Bunga Sahib

The Gurdwara is located in Baghat locality of Srinagar, Kashmir at a distance of 6km from the tourist reception centre.

This Gurdwara was constructed in the memory of Sikh soldiers of Maharaja Ranjit Singh during the period of Sikh rule in Kashmir (Click to read more)
